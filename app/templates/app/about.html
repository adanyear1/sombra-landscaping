Our History

While providing construction contracting services to clients, Nelson, Manuel, and Antero would frequently be asked if they would help clients with their landscaping needs. With no hesitation they would install beautiful outdoor lighting packages that would bring out the beauty of a ranch house during the night. Other clients would need help building a garden in their homes, so Nelson, Manuel, and Antero would work them to create the vision of their perfect garden. Frequently they would find themselves using their masonry skills to build brick flowerbeds in residential neighborhoods. After seeing the impact their work had on their clients' properties they decided to create Sombra Landscaping.

In the Summer of 2021, Sombra Landscaping was officially registered as an LLC in the state of Texas. Since then, the Sombra Landscaping team has worked with clients all over the Austin Metropolitan Area to help them create beautiful spaces within their homes. Sombra Landscaping has a strong portfolio of projects, where the team has installed palm trees for a resort style home, built fences for 5-acre properties, and turned a dull backyard into a tropical getaway. For the future, Sombra Landscaping plans on growing its team to better serve the fast-growing Austin area.
