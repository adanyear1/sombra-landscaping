About Sombra Landscaping

In early 2021, three construction professionals Manuel, Antero, and Nelson came together to unite their landscaping, concrete, masonry, and customer service skills. Clients who had visions of lush gardens and tropical backyards would see that these three professionals had an eye for detail, timeliness, and provided high quality work started to ask them if they would help them build them create their dreamscape. Due to customer demand they united forces and started Sombra Landscaping.

Why Sombra Landscaping?

The Sombra Landscaping team has a combined 30 years of experience working with customers in the Austin area with expertise in providing a myriad of construction services such as concrete, masonry, and landscaping. With an eye for detail, the Sombra Landscaping team ensures that our services leave your home looking beautiful by bringing out its natural beauty. What makes Sombra Landscaping stand out is that we ensure that our clients properties look better than before, and we ensure that our clients properties stay looking beautiful by providing recurring services.

We have established a relationship with Community Gardens in Bastrop, TX to be their customer support provider and help execute the visions of their clients
